Delete Account

You can request deletion of your Learnify Pakistan account using the steps below.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Learnify Pakistan app.
  2. Go to Account Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account (or contact us if the option is not visible).
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If you cannot access the app

Email us from your registered email address with:

  • Your username
  • Your full name
  • Your school (optional)
  • Request: “Delete my account”

Email: learnifypakistan81@gmail.com

What happens after deletion

  • Your account access will be removed.
  • Your personal profile data will be deleted from our system.
  • Basic records may be retained only if legally required (e.g., audit/security logs).

Effective Date: 2026
